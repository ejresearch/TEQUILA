WEEK 1 TOPICS – Introduction to Latin and Pronunciation
Grammar Focus – Nouns as naming words; introduction to endings (-a).
Skill Goals – Recognize Latin alphabet and sounds; learn five core vocabulary words; understand what nouns do.
Faith Phrase – Pax vobiscum (Peace be with you).
Virtue – Fides (Faith).
Mastery Indicator – Student can pronounce Latin letters and vocabulary and define noun as naming word.
