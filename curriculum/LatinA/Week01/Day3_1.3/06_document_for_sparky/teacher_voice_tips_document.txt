TEACHER VOICE TIPS – Day 3
Tone – patient, confident, and rhythmic.
Use slow repetition for translation sentences.
Alternate between Latin and English aloud to build comprehension.
Encourage full-body gestures for -a and -am.
Emphasize faith link: order in language = order in creation.
Praise warmly: “Pulchre! Ordinātē! Pax vobiscum!”
