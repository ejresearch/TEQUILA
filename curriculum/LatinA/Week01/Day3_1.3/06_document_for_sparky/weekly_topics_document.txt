WEEK 1 TOPICS – Practicing Endings
Grammar Focus – First declension nouns (singular); subject vs. object review.
Skill Goals – Identify correct form (-a, -am); translate short Latin sentences.
Faith Phrase – Pax vobiscum – Peace be with you.
Virtue – Fides leading to Ordō (trust turns to order).
Mastery Indicator – Student translates and identifies noun jobs correctly in 3–4 example sentences.
