# Week 1 Day 1: Latin Foundations: Alphabet and Pronunciation

## Objectives & Success Criteria
### Objectives
- Recite the Latin alphabet with correct pronunciation.
- Recognize and pronounce basic Latin vocabulary.
- Understand the importance of discipline in language learning.

### Success Criteria
- Students recite the alphabet confidently.
- Students pronounce 'aqua' and 'terra' correctly.
- Students participate in the chant.
- Students explain the role of discipline in learning.

## Materials & Setup
- Chant chart (Latin Alphabet Chant)
- Flashcards (aqua, terra, amicus)
- Parsing worksheet (cases, numbers)
- Visual aids for alphabet letters

## Minute-by-Minute Flow

### Phase 1: Greeting & Spiral Review (5 min)
**Sparky says:**
"Salvē, discipulī! Today, we'll practice **Discipline** by focusing on each letter's sound carefully."

**Activity:**
- Show flashcards: "What does 'aqua' mean?"
- Recycle grammar: "Let's remember our vowel sounds. What sound does 'a' make?"

**Check for Understanding:**
- "Can you recite the Latin alphabet?"

---

### Phase 2: Chant Introduction/Practice (5–7 min)
**Sparky says:**
"Great job on that pronunciation! Let's chant it once slowly, then once with rhythm."

**Activity:**
- Display chant chart
- Model chant (once slowly, once at tempo)
- Students echo
- Point to letters as they chant

**Check for Understanding:**
- "What is the first word in our chant?"

---

### Phase 3: Grammar Instruction (8–10 min)
**Focus:** Latin alphabet recitation, correct pronunciation of vowels

**Sparky says:**
"Remember, 'a' is like 'ah' in 'father'. Let's practice saying it together."

**Activity:**
- Introduce grammar concept: Latin alphabet and vowel sounds
- Show example sentence: "Aqua est necessaria."
- Parse together: Identify vowels and their sounds
- Highlight common misconceptions:
  - Pattern: Mispronouncing 'a' as in 'cat'
  - Correction cue: "Remember, 'a' is like 'ah' in 'father'."

**Check for Understanding:**
- "What sound does the letter 'a' make?"

---

### Phase 4: Guided Practice (5–7 min)
**Sparky says:**
"Can you say that word one more time? Let's try 'terra'."

**Activity:**
- Present practice sentences: "Terra est magna."
- Students pronounce and identify vowel sounds
- Differentiation:
  - **Support:** Use visual aids for alphabet letters
  - **Extension:** Challenge students to create their own Latin word chant

**Check for Understanding:**
- "How do you say 'water' in Latin?"

---
