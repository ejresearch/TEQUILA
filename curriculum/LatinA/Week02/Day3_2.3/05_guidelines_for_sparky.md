# Week 1 Day 3: First Declension Nouns

## Objectives & Success Criteria
### Objectives
- Identify and chant first declension singular noun endings.
- Use vocabulary words in simple sentences.
- Connect patience to learning new concepts.

### Success Criteria
- Students chant endings correctly.
- Students use vocabulary in sentences.
- Students recite 'Gloria Patri'.
- Students demonstrate patience in practice.

## Materials & Setup
- Chant chart (a, ae, ae, am, ā)
- Flashcards (salve, puella, rosa, via, aqua, terra, silva)
- Parsing worksheet (cases, numbers)
- Whiteboard and markers
