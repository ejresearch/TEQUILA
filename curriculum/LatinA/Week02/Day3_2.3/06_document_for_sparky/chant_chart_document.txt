First Declension Noun Chant:

Sing to the tune of 'Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star':

"a, ae, ae, am, ā,
First declension, here we say,
Nominative, Genitive, Dative too,
Accusative and Ablative, we review.

With motions:
- 'a' (raise right hand)
- 'ae' (raise left hand)
- 'ae' (touch head)
- 'am' (touch shoulders)
- 'ā' (touch knees)
