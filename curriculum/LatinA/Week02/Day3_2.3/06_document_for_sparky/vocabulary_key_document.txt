VOCABULARY KEY – Week 0 Core First Declension Nouns

puella – girl (puellarity, puelline)
rosa – rose (rosary, roseate)
via – road, way (viaduct, deviate)
aqua – water (aquatic, aquarium)
terra – earth, land (terrestrial, terrain)
silva – forest (sylvan, Pennsylvania)
