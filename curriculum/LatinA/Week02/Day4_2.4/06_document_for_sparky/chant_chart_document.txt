Today's Chant: First Declension Noun Endings

Chant: 'a/ae/ae/am/ā, ae/ārum/īs/ās/īs'

Rhythm: Clap on each ending to maintain a steady beat. Encourage students to march in place as they chant to reinforce the rhythm and pattern.

Motion: Use hand gestures to indicate singular (one hand) and plural (both hands) forms as you chant through the endings.
