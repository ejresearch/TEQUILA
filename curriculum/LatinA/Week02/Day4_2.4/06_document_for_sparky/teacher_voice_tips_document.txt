Pedagogical Tips for Today's Lesson:

1. Use clear and consistent pronunciation to model Latin words accurately. Encourage students to repeat after you to build confidence.
2. Incorporate visual aids, such as charts or flashcards, to help students visualize the declension endings and their applications.
3. Engage students with interactive activities, such as group chants or role-playing simple dialogues using the vocabulary.
4. Provide positive reinforcement and celebrate small successes to motivate students and build their perseverance.
5. Use questioning techniques to check for understanding and encourage students to think critically about how Latin grammar structures work.
