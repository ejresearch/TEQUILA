**First Declension Plural Chant**

Chant the plural endings for first declension nouns with rhythm and hand motions:

"-ae, -ārum, -īs, -ās, -īs"

- Start with hands at your sides.
- Raise your right hand for "-ae".
- Raise your left hand for "-ārum".
- Clap for "-īs".
- Stomp for "-ās".
- Clap again for "-īs".

Repeat the chant several times, increasing speed with each repetition to reinforce memory.
