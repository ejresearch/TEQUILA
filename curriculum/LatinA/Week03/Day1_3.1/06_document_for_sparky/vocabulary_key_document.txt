VOCABULARY KEY – Week 3 Core: First Declension Nouns in Plural

puella – girl (puellarity)
stella – star (stellar, constellation)
luna – moon (lunar, lunatic)
porta – gate (portal, port)
femina – woman (feminine, female)
nauta – sailor (nautical, astronaut)
salve – hello (salutation)
rosa – rose (rosary, roseate)
