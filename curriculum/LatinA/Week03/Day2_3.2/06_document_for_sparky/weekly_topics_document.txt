This week focuses on mastering the plural forms of first declension nouns. Students will learn to identify and use the plural endings: -ae, -훮rum, -카s, -훮s, -카s. The skill goal is to accurately decline first declension nouns in plural forms and use them in simple sentences. The virtue of diligence will be emphasized, encouraging students to apply consistent effort in their studies. Mastery will be indicated by students' ability to correctly decline and translate first declension nouns in plural.
