**Teacher Voice Tips for Latin Instruction**

1. **Clarity and Consistency**: Use clear and consistent pronunciation for Latin words. Model the correct pronunciation and have students repeat after you.

2. **Engagement through Rhythm**: Use chants and rhythmic clapping to engage students and aid memorization. Encourage students to participate actively in the chants.

3. **Connect to English**: Highlight English derivatives to help students see the relevance of Latin. This can aid in vocabulary retention and understanding.

4. **Encourage Diligence**: Remind students of the virtue of diligence. Praise their efforts and progress, reinforcing the importance of consistent practice.

5. **Visual Aids**: Use visual aids such as charts and diagrams to illustrate declension endings and noun cases. This can help visual learners grasp the concepts more effectively.
