**Week 3 Overview**

*Grammar Focus*: First Declension Nouns in Plural

*Skill Goals*:
- Mastery of first declension noun endings in plural
- Ability to translate simple Latin sentences using plural nouns
- Recognition of Latin noun cases in plural

*Virtue*: Diligence

*Mastery Indicator*: Students will accurately decline first declension nouns in the plural form and use them in simple Latin sentences.
