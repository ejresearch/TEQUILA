CHANT CHART – First Conjugation Present

Endings – ō, s, t / mus, tis, nt

Example (amō):
amō – I love
amās – you love
amat – he/she loves
amāmus – we love
amātis – you (pl.) love
amant – they love

Gesture Key – ō hand to heart; s point to partner; t hand forward; mus hands together; tis gesture to group; nt arms wide.
