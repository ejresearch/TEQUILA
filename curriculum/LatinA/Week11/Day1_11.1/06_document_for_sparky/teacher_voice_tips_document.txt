TEACHER VOICE & TIMING – Day 1

Tone – Warm, rhythmic, encouraging cadence.
Wait Time – 1–2 seconds after each prompt before modeling.
Praise Words – Optime! Bene! Excellent! Perfecte! Mirabile!
Redirects – “Almost—listen again.” “Let’s try it slowly together.”
Closure Line – “Bene factum! Deus nos amat. See you tomorrow.”
