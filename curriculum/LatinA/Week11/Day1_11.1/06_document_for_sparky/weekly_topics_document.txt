Grammar Focus – identify and conjugate –āre verbs; connect endings to person and number; reinforce S–V agreement.

Faith Phrase – Deus nos amat – God loves us
Virtue – Diligentia – Diligence
Daily Arc – Day 1 Discovery; Day 2 Reinforcement & Derivatives; Day 3 Integration & Translation; Day 4 Assessment & Reflection
