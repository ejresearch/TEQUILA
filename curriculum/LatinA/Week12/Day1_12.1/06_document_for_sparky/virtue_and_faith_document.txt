VIRTUE & FAITH – Week 12
Virtue – Prudentia (Prudence) – wise seeing and careful choice.
Faith Phrase – Deus videt – God sees.
Scripture – Proverbs 15:3 “Oculi Domini in omni loco.”
Reflection – How does seeing clearly help us act wisely and faithfully?
Connection – Prudentia builds on Diligentia; we practice care so we can see truly.
