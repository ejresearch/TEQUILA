WEEK 12 – Second Conjugation –ēre Verbs (Present Tense)
Grammar Focus – recognize and chant Second Conjugation endings –eō, –ēs, –et / –ēmus, –ētis, –ent.
Core Verbs – videō (see), moneō (warn), teneō (hold), habeō (have), timeō (fear).
Faith Phrase – Deus videt – God sees.
Virtue – Prudentia – Prudence.
Mastery Indicators – Student chants new endings accurately and explains differences from –āre family.
