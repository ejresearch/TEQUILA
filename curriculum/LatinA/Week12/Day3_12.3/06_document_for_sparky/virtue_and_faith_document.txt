VIRTUE & FAITH – Day 3 Reflection
Virtue – Prudentia (Prudence) – wise seeing and careful choice.
Faith Phrase – Deus videt – God sees.
Scripture – Proverbs 15:3 “Oculi Domini in omni loco.”
Reflection – How does seeing truth in Latin help us see truth in life?
Connection – Prudentia grows from Diligentia: we practice care so we can see clearly.
