VIRTUE & FAITH – Week 13 Opening
Virtue – Veritas (Truth) – to speak and see what is real.
Faith Phrase – Verbum Dei verum est – The Word of God is true.
Scripture – Psalm 33:4 “The word of the Lord is upright.”
Reflection – How does truthful speech show wisdom and faith?
Connection – Truth builds on Prudence; seeing clearly helps us speak truly.
