TEACHER VOICE & TIMING – Day 2
Tone – warm and steady; guide students to notice endings.
Pacing – use pause after each translation.
Praise Words – Verum! Optime! Bene dixisti!
Redirect – “Who receives the action? Look for –am or –um.”
Closure – “Today you spoke true Latin sentences that show who does and who receives the action. Verbum Dei verum est.”
