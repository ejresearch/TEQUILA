VIRTUE & FAITH – Day 2 Reflection
Virtue – Veritas (Truth) – speaking what is real and precise.
Faith Phrase – Verbum Dei verum est – The Word of God is true.
Scripture – Psalm 33:4 “The word of the Lord is upright.”
Reflection – How does truth in words help us show care in study and life?
Connection – Truth is built on Prudence: see clearly, then speak clearly.
