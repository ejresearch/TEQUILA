VIRTUE & FAITH – Day 3 Reflection
Virtue – Veritas (Truth) – joining what is right and real.
Faith Phrase – Verbum Dei verum est – The Word of God is true.
Scripture – Psalm 33:4 “The word of the Lord is upright.”
Reflection – Truth joins words rightly, just as est joins ideas rightly.
Connection – Truth grows from Prudence; it makes right connections.
