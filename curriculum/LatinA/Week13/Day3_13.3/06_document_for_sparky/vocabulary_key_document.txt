VOCABULARY KEY – Sentence Patterns C & D
Pattern C – Linking Verb: est (is)
Adjectives: bonus, bona, bonum (good); magnus (great); parvus (small).
Pattern D – Indirect Object (dative): servō (to/for the servant), puerō (to/for the boy), puellae (to/for the girl).
Nouns: dominus (lord), donum (gift).
Verbs: dat (gives), est (is).
