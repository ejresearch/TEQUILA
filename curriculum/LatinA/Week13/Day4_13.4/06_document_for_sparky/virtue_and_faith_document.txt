VIRTUE & FAITH – Week 13 Closing
Virtue – Veritas (Truth) – faithful seeing and speaking.
Faith Phrase – Verbum Dei verum est – The Word of God is true.
Scripture – Psalm 33:4 “The word of the Lord is upright.”
Reflection – Truth joins words and actions rightly; study with honesty brings light.
Connection – Truth completes the virtue chain and prepares for next week’s growth.
