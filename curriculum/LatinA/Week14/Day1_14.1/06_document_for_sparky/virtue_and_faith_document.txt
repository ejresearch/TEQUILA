VIRTUE & FAITH – Week 14 Opening
Virtue – Ordō (Order) – God orders all things rightly; our words should too.
Faith Phrase – Ad Dominum currimus – We run to the Lord.
Scripture – Psalm 119:133 “Direct my steps by Your word.”
Reflection – Order in grammar mirrors order in creation and faith.
Connection – Truth (Veritas) shows what is right; Order (Ordō) places it rightly.
