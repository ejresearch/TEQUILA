VIRTUE & FAITH – Day 2 Reflection
Virtue – Ordō (Order) – Right direction brings peace.
Faith Phrase – Ad Dominum currimus – We run to the Lord.
Scripture – Psalm 119:133 “Direct my steps by Your word.”
Reflection – Every Latin ending shows order; every faithful step follows God’s direction.
Connection – Truth speaks rightly (Veritas); Order walks rightly (Ordō).
