CHANT CHART – Ordered Sentences
Puella currit ad domum – The girl runs to the house.
Puer portat aquam per viam – The boy carries water through the road.
Agricola ambulat in agrum – The farmer walks into the field.
Dominus stetit ante domum – The master stood before the house.
Chant rhythm: “Each step in place, each word in line; Latin order, truth divine!”
