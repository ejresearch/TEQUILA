VIRTUE & FAITH – Day 3 Reflection
Virtue – Ordō (Order) – Following God’s direction in all things.
Faith Phrase – Ad Dominum currimus – We run to the Lord.
Scripture – Psalm 119:133 “Direct my steps by Your word.”
Reflection – Just as words must follow their right order, our hearts must follow God’s order.
Connection – Truth shows what is; Order shows how all fits together.
