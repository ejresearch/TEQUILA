VIRTUE & FAITH – Week 15 Opening
Virtue – Pax (Peace) – Calm and harmony following order.
Faith Phrase – Cum Deō ambulāmus – We walk with God.
Scripture – John 14:27 “Peace I leave with you; My peace I give to you.”
Reflection – Peace is right placement: everything resting where it belongs.
Connection – After Order (Ordō) comes Peace (Pax).
