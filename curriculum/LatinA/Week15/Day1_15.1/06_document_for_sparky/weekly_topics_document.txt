WEEK 15 – Prepositions with the Ablative (Rest & Relationship)
Grammar Focus – Ablative case for rest, location, and accompaniment.
Skill Goal – Recognize and translate Ablative prepositional phrases.
Faith Phrase – Cum Deō ambulāmus – We walk with God.
Virtue – Pax – Peace.
Mastery Indicator – Student distinguishes motion (Accusative) from rest (Ablative).
