VIRTUE & FAITH – Day 2 Reflection
Virtue – Pax (Peace) – Calm that comes from right placement.
Faith Phrase – Cum Deō ambulāmus – We walk with God.
Scripture – John 14:27 “Peace I leave with you; My peace I give to you.”
Reflection – In grammar and in life, peace comes when everything rests where it belongs.
Connection – Order (Ordō) prepares for Peace (Pax).
