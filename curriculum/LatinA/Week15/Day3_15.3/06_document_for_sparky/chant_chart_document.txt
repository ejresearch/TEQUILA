CHANT CHART – Peaceful Sentences
Puella sedet in hortō – The girl sits in the garden.
Puer habitat cum amīcō – The boy lives with a friend.
Agricola est sub arbore – The farmer is under the tree.
Sacerdōs venit ē templō – The priest comes out of the temple.
Chant rhythm: “Rest with peace, all in place – Ablative endings show the space!”
