VIRTUE & FAITH – Day 3 Reflection
Virtue – Pax (Peace) – Balance between motion and rest.
Faith Phrase – Cum Deō ambulāmus – We walk with God.
Scripture – John 14:27 “Peace I leave with you; My peace I give to you.”
Reflection – Peace comes from harmony: action rightly joined with rest.
Connection – After Order (Ordō) comes Peace (Pax) through right relationship.
